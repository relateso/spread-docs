## Welcome to Spread!
Welcome to Spread! This is your first step to using Spread. This Onboarding guide walks through the steps to kickstart setting up email for your B2B company.

## What is Spread?
<img
  className="block dark:hidden"
  src="/images/hero-light.png"

/>
<img
  className="hidden dark:block"

Spread is a simple email sending platform for B2B companies. With Spread, you can:

- Send marketing and product update emails to your customers
- Send transactional emails to your users
- Create beautiful emails with a markdown editor
- Schedule emails to send at the right time
- Track email opens, clicks, and more
- Build lists to track your customers, users, and more.
